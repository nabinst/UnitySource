Future Programs

The after school program provides students in grades K-12 with a safe, caring place to play, learn life skills, enjoy enrichment activities and receive academic assistance. The youth leadership program develops and utilizes leadership skills and instills responsibility. A variety age appropriate experiences and trips augment the students development. Seasonal family events assist in building a stronger community. Unity Center firmly believes in equality and therefore welcomes students of various races, ethnicities and religious and non-religious affiliations.
