Youth Leadership

Youth in grades 7-12 fulfill essential leadership roles in the day to day operation of the FUTURE After School Program. The DAWN youth group (Disciples Acting With Nobility) meets weekly at the Melting Pot for enrichment and development and to plan and carry out service projects and social events. Approximately every 3 to 4 years, youth leaders are selected to participate in a week-long trip to Washington D.C. for learning seminars and touring.
