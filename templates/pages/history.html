Our History

2017

2017-Present

Purchased and renovated adjoining property and house "House of Dreams" for expanded programing, began partnership with United Way of Ocoee Region and AmeriCorps VISTA program, 1st Nashville Trek Enrichment trip for 4th graders, began Story Catchers program, average attendance 92

2011

2011-2016

Added snack preparation volunteers, became members of TIRRC (Tennessee Immigration Refugee Rights Coalition), renovated Melting Pot for office space and youth and adult programming, opened playfield, built addition onto Home Place (commercial kitchen, accessible bathrooms, storage), 1st Tellus Science Museum Trip for 3rd graders, average attendance 90

2006

2006-2010

Renovation of Basement and upstairs bathrooms and entrance of Community Building, 1st Dress for Success, 1st Youth Leader trip to Washington DC, acquired adjoining property for development, added piano lab, DAWN Youth group organized, began affiliation with Lee University to utilize students as tutors, added 4th day, average attendance 60

2000

2000-2005

1st Community Egg Hunt, 1st Community Choir Program, 1st Skating Party, 1st Teacher Tea, Tutor Training Program began, added 3rd Day, Wonders in the Woods Unity Tent Camps began, renovated sanctuary for usable student space, average attendance 45

1999

Begain the Journey

In 1999, Unity Center began to offer summer day camps and the FUTURE 2-day per week after school program with an average of 15 students.
